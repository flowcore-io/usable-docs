## What is Usable?

Usable is an AI-powered long-term memory system that helps development teams document, discover, and share knowledge seamlessly. With Usable, your codebase becomes a living knowledge base that grows smarter over time.

## Features

### Long-Term Memory
Store verified insights, solutions, and patterns that persist across coding sessions. Your AI assistant remembers what worked before.

### Fragment Types
Organize knowledge with purpose-built fragment types: Knowledge, Recipe, Solution, Template, and more.

### Team Collaboration
Share workspaces, invite team members, and build a collective knowledge base that everyone can access.

### Privacy & Security
Control who sees what with workspace permissions, role-based access control, and private fragments.
